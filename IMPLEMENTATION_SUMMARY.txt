DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Colors Used:
  Icon:     #7dd3fc  (cyan, 80% opacity)
  Title:    #e8f0fd  (light blue)
  Text:     #d1dce9  (muted blue)
  Divider:  Gradient fade (starts 20%, ends 5% opacity)

  Icon:     18px × 18px, flex-shrink: 0
  Title:    0.95rem, 600 weight, 0.02em letter-spacing
  Text:     0.94rem, 1.65 line-height

Animation:
  Duration: 0.6s
  Easing:   ease-out
  Motion:   Fade (0→100%) + Slide (8px down → 0px)
